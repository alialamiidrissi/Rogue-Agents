Single Panel Comic

Observe, young lad! This apple, released from my grasp, plummets not by whim or chance, but by an unseen, undeniable force! It is the Universal Law of Gravitation! Every particle attracts every other particle with a force proportional to the product of their masses and inversely proportional to the square of the distance between their centers! Do you comprehend? The very cosmos is bound by this elegant, immutable dance!
So... the apple... it 'likes' the Earth? And the Earth... it 'likes' the apple back? And 'inversely proportional to the square of the distance'... does that mean if I stand on the moon, the apple 'likes' the Earth less? My quill is confused, Sir Newton, much like my very soul!
