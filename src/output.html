The Great Green Energy Adventure!

Join Lily and Leo as they shrink down to see plants' sweet secrets!

Hello! I'm Professor Sprout! To make my food, I need sunlight, water, and CO2!
And I'm Solar Sam, providing the essential energy for the process!
With Solar Sam's energy, I convert these raw materials into yummy sugars... my very own food!
My light helps activate the chlorophyll in your cells, turning that CO2 into energy!
And as a gift back to the world, I release fresh oxygen for everyone to breathe! Everyone wins!
That's why photosynthesis is the ultimate life-sustaining process on Earth! Go, plants!
